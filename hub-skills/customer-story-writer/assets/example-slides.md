<div class="col">
<div class="box">
<ul>
<li>AstraZeneca's Incident Response Team is slowed by a <strong>60+ page Business Continuity Plan</strong> nobody can navigate during an actual event</li>
<li>New Project Manager onboarding takes too long with <strong>lessons learned trapped in SharePoint folders</strong> nobody searches</li>
<li>Supply chain team handling <strong>1,500+ monthly queries from 200+ users</strong> manually</li>
<li>Some problem from the upcoming marketing content creation team</li>
</ul>
<div class="narr">
<p>Five separate business units came to the DC Hub with separate problems; all rooted in the same issue: institutional knowledge locked in documents that people cannot find when they need it.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="col">
<div class="box">
<div class="narr" style="margin-top: 0; padding-top: 0; border: none;">
<p>AstraZeneca is driving AI agent adoption globally. The Washington DC Hub coordinated with the London Hub and AstraZeneca account team to extend Microsoft's strategy to local teams in the US.</p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>The strategy kicked off with an <strong>Architecture Design Session</strong> to confirm the prioritized use cases and customer sponsorship</li>
<li>We recommended a <strong>hands-on prototyping approach</strong> &#x2014; this team had the technical maturity to build, they needed architectural guidance to go from experiments to production</li>
<li>We paired a dedicated Microsoft engineer with each AstraZeneca business owner across <strong>parallel build tracks</strong>, putting their teams directly in Copilot Studio</li>
</ul>
<div class="narr">
<p>The approach validated itself: the workshop created internal demand across the organization. Teams outside the original heard what was built and asked for their own sessions.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="col">

<li><strong>80% adoption</strong> by Project Managers of lessons learned within 6 months</li>
<li>Supply chain: <strong>1,500+ monthly queries</strong> now accessible via natural language</li>
</ul>
